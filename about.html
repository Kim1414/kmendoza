BATANGAS

Batangas is a province in the Philippines located in the south-western part of Luzon in the CALABARZON region. It is surrounded by Cavite and Laguna in the north, Quezon in the east, Mindoro in the south, and the South China Sea in the west.

Because of its coastal geography, Batangas is famous for its beaches, marine life, and rich flora. The province has 31 municipalities and 3 cities. Batangueños speak Tagalog with a deep and melodic accent, while English is widely understood.

Known for its historical contributions, beautiful beaches, and warm people, Batangas is one of the most popular travel destinations in the Philippines— just 2 to 3 hours away from Manila.
